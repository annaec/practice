PRACTICE

An assertion of sympathetic magic

PRACTICE is the third in an ongoing series of Boston-based art exhibits that turn away from sanctioned art making and showing. PRACTICE features artists from New York, Boston, and LA, and aims to reanimate the customs behind sympathetic magic by asking artists to inject a meaning, memory, or purpose into a physical object to reflect the ritualist power of correspondence, imitation, and heredity.

Practice opens at 7pm on December 31, 2017 at The Distillery Gallery in South Boston, MA. Work will be on display through January 27, 2018.
